LoRAbit

LoRAbit helps organize an MLX-based local LoRA workflow: prepare datasets, inspect model folders, run preflight checks, train adapters, and export results from one workspace.

What LoRAbit is for

  • Conservative preflight checks before training
  • MLX adapter and inference-ready export organization
